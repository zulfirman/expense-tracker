+# Recommendations for Family Budget Tracker
+
+This app is designed for couples (husband/wife) to monitor and budget their salary together. Here are simple, practical recommendations to make it perfect for family use.
+
+## ðŸŽ¯ Essential Features for Couples
+
+### 1. **Monthly Budget Planning**
+- Set monthly budget limits for each category
+- Track spending against budget with visual progress bars
+- Get alerts when approaching budget limits (50%, 75%, 100%)
+- **Why**: Helps couples stay within their monthly salary limits
+
+### 2. **Shared View & Sync**
+- Both partners can see the same data in real-time
+- Simple sync mechanism (cloud or local network)
+- **Why**: Transparency and accountability between partners
+
+### 3. **Quick Expense Entry**
+- Fast entry for common daily expenses
+- Voice input for hands-free entry
+- Receipt photo capture with auto-amount detection
+- **Why**: Makes tracking easy, so couples actually use it daily
+
+### 4. **Monthly Summary Reports**
+- Simple monthly summary: Income vs Expenses
+- Category breakdown showing where money went
+- Visual charts showing spending trends
+- **Why**: Helps couples understand their spending patterns
+
+### 5. **Goal Setting**
+- Set savings goals (vacation, emergency fund, etc.)
+- Track progress together
+- Celebrate milestones
+- **Why**: Motivates couples to save together
+
+## ðŸš€ Nice-to-Have Features
+
+### 6. **Recurring Expenses**
+- Set up monthly bills (rent, utilities, subscriptions)
+- Auto-schedule recurring expenses
+- **Why**: Saves time on repetitive entries
+
+### 7. **Export & Backup**
+- Export monthly reports to PDF
+- Backup data to cloud (Google Drive, iCloud)
+- **Why**: Keep records safe and shareable
+
+### 8. **Spending Alerts**
+- Daily reminders to log expenses
+- Budget threshold alerts
+- Monthly summary notifications
+- **Why**: Keeps both partners engaged
+
+### 9. **Simple Search**
+- Search expenses by date, category, or notes
+- Filter by date range
+- **Why**: Quick access to past expenses
+
+### 10. **Dark Mode**
+- Easy on the eyes for evening use
+- Matches device settings
+- **Why**: Better user experience
+
+## ðŸ’¡ Design Principles
+
+- **Keep it simple**: No complex features that confuse users
+- **Fast entry**: Make logging expenses quick and easy
+- **Visual feedback**: Charts and progress bars help understand spending
+- **Mobile-first**: Most entries happen on phones
+- **Offline support**: Works without internet
+
+## ðŸ“Š Success Metrics
+
+- Both partners log expenses daily
+- Monthly budget is followed
+- Savings goals are achieved
+- Couples feel more in control of their finances
+
+---
+
+**Priority**: Focus on features 1-5 first. These provide the most value for couples managing their salary together.
